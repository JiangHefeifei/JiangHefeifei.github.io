      About

      Hi! I am Hefeifei Jiang (姜何飞飞), a Master's student in Robotics at the University of California, Riverside, where I work at the Trustworthy Autonomous Systems Laboratory (TASL), advised by Professor Jiachen Li.

      My research centers on embodied AI and robot learning — spanning vision-language agents, multi-agent systems, and reinforcement learning for autonomous vehicles and mobile robots. I am drawn to building agents that perceive, reason about, and safely act in the physical world alongside people.

      Earlier, I earned a first-class B.Eng. in Computer Science through a joint program of Shaanxi University of Science & Technology and Ulster University.

      I am applying to PhD programs. Feel free to reach out — I am always happy to chat about research.
